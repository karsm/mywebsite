VOCABULARY

the body of words used in a particular language.

This website can help you learn new words, play games that improve your vocabulary, and explore language.

How to improve your vocabulary?

Set a goal. If you are commited to building your vocabulary, set a goal for yourself. Try to learn three new words a week and work them into your speech and writing. With concious effort, you can learn several thousand of new words that you'll remember and use.
